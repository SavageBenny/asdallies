About Us

ASD Allies is a grassroots nonprofit fighting to help families afford the autism support that insurance won’t cover.

We fundraise to provide financial assistance for therapies, supplements, equipment, and alternative treatments that are often left out by the system. Whether it’s ABA, gut protocols, assistive tech, or healing practices that make a difference — we step in where others won’t.

We’re parents, friends, and fighters who have lived it — and we believe every child deserves access to the tools that help them thrive.

What We Do

  • Financial assistance for out-of-pocket autism support
  • Educational content for parents and caregivers
  • Guides to therapies, supplements, and alternative paths
  • Database of resources, tools, and community connections
